# Structural Specifications - Block A

## 1. Concrete Work
- Foundations: Grade 30 concrete with sulfate-resistant cement.
- Columns and Beams: Grade 35 concrete with high-yield reinforcement.

## 2. Steelwork
- All structural steel to be hot-dip galvanized.
- Welding as per BS EN 1011.

## 3. Masonry
- External walls: 200mm hollow concrete blocks.
- Internal partitions: 100mm solid concrete blocks.
